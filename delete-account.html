Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Frø account and all associated data at any time.

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Your seed collection stored locally on your device will not be affected — it is deleted when you uninstall the app.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to contact@frø.app from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account deletion request
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email when it is complete.

What gets deleted

  • Your account — email address, name, and sign-in credentials
  • Shared seeds — all seed records you have shared with other growers via QR code
  • Community growing events — all anonymized planting events contributed to the community map

What is not affected

  • Your local seed collection — stored only on your device. It is not deleted by an account deletion request. To remove it, uninstall the app.
  • Seeds others have imported from your shares — once a recipient has imported a shared seed into their own collection, that copy is theirs and is not removed.
